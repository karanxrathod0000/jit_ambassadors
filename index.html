Your Turn & Next Steps

Using AI Responsibly and Joining the Community

Prompt Like a Pro

Great prompts have key ingredients. The more detail you provide, the better your results will be.

Give a role, define the goal, specify the audience, set context, and format the result.

Helpful Phrases to Add:

  • "Explain [topic] to me like I'm five."
  • "Create a quiz to test my understanding of [topic]."
  • "Give me pros and cons of [topic]."
  • "Transform the key points of [topic] into a story."

Use Your Powers for Good

Great power comes with great responsibility. Use AI smartly and ethically.

DON'T Plagiarize

Use it to brainstorm, learn, and refine YOUR own work.

DO Fact-Check

AI can "hallucinate." Always verify critical information from reliable sources.

DO Cite Your Tool

Ask your professor for their policy on using AI tools.
